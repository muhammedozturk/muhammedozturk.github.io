He completed his elementary and high education in Isparta. He graduated from Pamukkale University computer engineering department in 2008. He attended Turkish army for compulsory military service as tank lieutenant between 2009-2010. He then worked in Keytorc firm as software tester for one year. He took master and doctorate degree from Sakarya University computer engineering department 2012 and 2016 respectively. Currently he is working in department of computer engineering in Suleyman Demirel University. His phd thesis title is "improved defect prediction for software development using repeated data analysis"
